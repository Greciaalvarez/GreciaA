1. Research Framework

This independent research and development project focuses on the creation of an algorithmic system designed to analyze financial markets and evaluate trading risk through quantitative rules and systematic backtesting.

2. System Architecture

The architecture is designed to minimize emotional bias and maximize algorithmic discipline:

  • Directional Bias (H4 timeframe): EMA (20/50) crossovers.
  • Execution Layer (H1 timeframe): Pullback and Resumption model (RSI & ATR).
  • Risk Management Engine: Dynamic position sizing and ATR-based stop-loss.
